Wish to Volunteer with a Team of Talented Musicians?

 
 

Are you a budding or former musician looking for a place to learn, grow, and excel? Would you like to spend time with the phenomenal passionate people? Would you like the opportunity to bring music to your own local community? Then consider joining The Tidewater Concert band.

The Tidewater Concert Band is open to wind players and percussionists of high school age and up. An interview and informal audition is required. School students should also have a recommendation from their band director. To set up an interview, please contact Chris Serano at 757-717-3111 or send us a message through this website.

Rehearsal Information
When:Every Tuesday Evening | 7:30pm.

Where: Fellowship Hall Calvary Baptist Church, 2117 London Blvd Portsmouth, VA 23704
