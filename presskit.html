Press Kit

 
 

The Tidewater Concert Band provides three primary shows (Patriotic, Traditional, Holiday) for Graduation Ceremonies as well as:

    Local Parades

    Family Celebrations

    Art Shows

    Festivals

    Retirement/Nursing Homes

    Concert series

The group always welcomes new performance opportunities in The Seven Cities.
