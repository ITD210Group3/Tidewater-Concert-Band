Want Tidewater Concert Band at your next event?

 
 

Since 1971, The Tidewater Concert Band had been providing exciting, professional, high quality music entertainment to Hampton Roads and the surrounding area. The Tidewater Concert Band can provide performances ranging from Sousa Style Marches, Big Band, to Swing, Blues, and even Broadway Musicals.

Currently under the direction of Maestro Chris Serrano and Associate Conductor Josiah Wilson, the band consists of volunteer musicians from every occupation including, local band directors, students, as well as former and current military musicians. With passion and determination these individual musicians come together to produce a fully-instrumented top-notch professional performing organization.

The Tidewater Concert Band is a cultural jewel for local audience members, musicians, venues, and event organizers alike.

We appreciate your visit. We thank you so much for your time and attention and we look forward to seeing you very soon.
